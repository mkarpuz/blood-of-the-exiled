# Background

1. An open-world, MMO-style action RPG with a cinematic camera and high-quality animations, playable in the browser.
2. It will include characters, leveling, skills, items, environments, cities, nature, monsters, bosses, and NPCs.
3. The goal is to help people learn things such as German, Python, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. In practice, it can be used to learn anything I or anyone else wants to study.
4. The existence of a large learning material repo that is fed to the game. 

# Qualities

1. High-quality movement
2. Flexibility
3. A strong environment
4. A good story
5. Good cinematics
6. Leveling up should make you feel stronger, with satisfying character progression
7. Fluid combat
8. The destination should not be obvious. Players should wander through a vast world until they discover it.
9. Players may rely heavily on the minimap while exploring and fighting.
10. Players will often teleport back to camp to sell gear, buy potions, and revive their reckless companion.
11. Camera view: third-person, over-the-shoulder perspective
